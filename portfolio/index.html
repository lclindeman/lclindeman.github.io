Fundraising Campaigns

I crafted the copy for Buckhead Christian Ministry's "12 Days of Christmas" fundraising campaign. It was BCM's first campaign of the sort, so we tracked analytics from this year to serve as a baseline to gauge future success.

I also created BCM's profile for Georgia Gives Day, a statewide fundraising event. We raised over $30,000 that day through strategically timed emails, tweets, and Facebook posts.
