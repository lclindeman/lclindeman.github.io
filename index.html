I spent two formative high-school years at The Mississippi School for Mathematics and Science before attending Birmingham-Southern College, where I graudated summa cum laude with a BA in English. I worked as a non-profit professional in Huntsville, AL and in Atlanta, GA, where I managed the office, tracked and acknowledged donations, and assisted with fundraising events. I cultivated a love for the tech community through my efforts to learn to code and currently work at The Iron Yard Atlanta as the Assistant Campus Director. I geek out over community building, scheduling, and event logistics, tweet and blog up a storm, and help students find jobs as the final piece of their life-changing experiene at The Iron Yard.

Offline I enjoy hiking and backpacking with my husband. The kitchen is my happy place, and I'm always trying out new recipes that I find on Pinterest. I'm a firm proponent of the Oxford comma and somewhat pedantic about the conventions of the 4-way stop. I seem to collect funky earrings and cardigans. I enjoy monthly meetings with my book club, where we sometimes talk about the book we've read (anything from pop fiction to literature to non-fiction!) but also drink wine and eat lots of cheese. You can usually find me at Rails Girls on the last Wednesday of the month.
